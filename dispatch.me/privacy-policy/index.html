DISPATCH PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: July 31, 2017

Dispatch Technologies, Inc. (Dispatch” us, our, or we) values your privacy. Dispatch provides on-demand services and real-time work force tracking solutions (the Dispatch Services) through our mobile applications (our Apps), our website at www.dispatch.me (Site), and for some businesses using our Dispatch Application Programming Interface (Dispatch API) to integrate with our systems.

This privacy policy (Privacy Policy) describes the type of information our Site, our Apps and the Dispatch Services collect from you, our practices with respect to the information we collect, and the steps we have taken to protect that information. By visiting our Site, or by using our Apps or the Dispatch Services, you are expressly consenting to the collection, use and disclosure of your Personal Information and User Information (as defined below) in accordance with this Privacy Policy, and you are agreeing to be bound by our Terms of Service. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY OR THE TERMS OF SERVICE, PLEASE DO NOT VISIT OUR SITE OR USE THE APPS OR THE DISPATCH SERVICES.

WHAT INFORMATION DOES DISPATCH COLLECT?

Dispatch collects certain information from (i) third party enterprise partners (Third Party Partners) using the Dispatch Services to facilitate and track job appointments, (ii) Service providers (Service Providers) using the Dispatch Services to manage information about and fulfill job appointments, (iii) Clients and customers (Customers) of Third Party Partners and Service Providers using a customer portal powered by Dispatch or hosted by a Third Party Partner or Service Provider (for example, to book a service appointment) (a Customer Portal), and (iv) visitors to our Site (collectively, Users, you or your). We use information collected from Users in accordance with this Privacy Policy to provide and enhance the quality of the Dispatch Services and for our internal business purposes.

Types Of Data We Collect.

Personal Information. Dispatch may collect certain personally identifiable information from Users, such as name, address, email address, business name, telephone number, zip code, and other contact or non-public information about an individual that is associated with or linked to any of the foregoing data. We call this information Personal Information. We dont share Personal Information with third parties except as specified in this Privacy Policy.
Third Party Information. From Third Party Partners we may additionally collect information about their current or future potential customers or clients, including information about past or future services jobs performed for these customers or clients.
App Information. Dispatch collects information from Third Party Partners and Service Providers about their use of our Apps. Such information may include language preference, phone number, the make and model of any phone or device used to access the Apps, the mobile operating system, the type of browser in use with the Apps, IP address or other unique mobile identifiers, and geolocation data. We call this information App Information.
Analytics Information. Dispatch has partnered with Google Analytics, FullStory and Pendo.io (collectively, Analytics Partners) to collect information about how our Site, Apps and the Dispatch Services are used. This data may be associated with or linked to Personal Information. You can learn more about these data collection practices by referring to the HOW ARE COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES USED section below.
Anonymous Information. We may automatically collect non-personal information about your use of the Dispatch Services, or our Site or Apps, through cookies, web beacons and other technologies as more fully described in the HOW ARE COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES USED section below. Such information may include domain name, browser and operating system type, web pages viewed, links clicked, IP address, duration of visit to our Site or use of our Apps, among other things. We call this information Anonymous Information. For clarity, Anonymous Information is data which are not associated with or linked to your Personal Information and which do not alone permit the identification of individual persons.

If you believe that an unauthorized access has already occurred or may occur please report it immediately to legal@dispatch.me. HOW ARE COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES USED?

Dispatchs Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies.

Dispatch may at its discretion use both session and persistent cookies or beacons on its Site or in our Apps. To help you understand these technologies better:

Cookies are electronic files many websites or applications place on a visitors computer or device to store information that makes each visit more efficient. A session cookie stores information about you only while you are interacting with a website or application, and is then deleted when the interaction stops. Persistent cookies store information about you, your preferences, and information about other websites you visit, and it remains on your computer or device. To remove cookies, you may configure your browser to clear all cookies, or configure it to reject cookies. Please consult your browsers user guide for specific instructions on removing cookies.

Beacons are small graphic files with a unique identifier that are used to track a web or mobile users online movements. Unlike cookies, beacons are embedded on websites or devices, not downloaded on your computer or device. Dispatch may use beacons to better manage content on our Site and to improve the use and efficiency of our Apps.

Our Analytics Partners named above may use cookies, beacons or other automated technologies to collect information about how many times you visit the Site or use our Apps, which parts of the Site and/or Apps you access, and the functionality you are using, among other data elements.
